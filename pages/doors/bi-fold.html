Bi-Fold Doors

Invite the outdoors in.

For an uninterrupted space between the great outdoors and your home.

With ultra slim frames there’s nothing to obscure your view or stop all that extra light flooding into the heart of your home.

Our patio bi-fold doors slide open with the lightest of touches and fold back flatter than any rival model. That means you can create a continuous ‘garden room’ that stretches form inside your home to the great outdoors in one single, uninterrupted space.

Benefits of Bi-Fold Doors:

Choice and flexibility

Fast, accurate installation

Bespoke British designed hardware

10-year guarantee for peace of mind

Style and performance you can enjoy for many years to come.

All our bi-fold doors are engineered to operate smoothly and reliably. They’re designed to look great too, so you can create an eye-catching feature for your home. We know every home is different so there’s plenty of options to help you customise your bi-fold doors

Understanding Door opening configurations

Other options - All options are available as inward and outward opening doors. Extra leaves and additional configurations can be chosen, up to the maximum width of six metres. *Maximum sash width per leaf is 900mm

Example - 2-2-0. 2 = Total number of leaves. 2 = Number of leaves folding left. 0 = Number of leaves folding right
